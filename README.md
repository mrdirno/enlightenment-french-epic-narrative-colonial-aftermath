## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a chapter from the perspective of Sergeant Bao, navigating loyalty between the colonial judge and his own people.
- Create a 'found document'—a letter from Claude Valois to his brother in the Chamber of Deputies, written before his death.
- Draft a scene where Élise Valois confronts the Parisian board members of the Société des Caoutchoucs.
- Write a folktale emerging from the local village about the 'stone god' and the river, reflecting the colonial conflict.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive map of the colony showing the rubber plantations, the river, the estate, and the mission, annotating key events.
- Create a 'ledger generator' that produces faux-colonial accounting documents showing the 'cost per kilo' of rubber, with hidden fields revealing the human toll.
